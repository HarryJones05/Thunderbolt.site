## TONE & VOICE GUIDELINES FOR THE SITE

- **Direct, not salesy.** State what the system does and what it replaces. No hype words like "revolutionary" or "game-changing."
- **Technical credibility, plain language.** Show you understand the tech (mention GHL, ManyChat, Claude API by name) but explain outcomes in business terms (booked calls, recovered revenue, hours saved).
- **Urgency without fear.** The "automate or get left behind" angle is honest, not manipulative. Back it with logic, not pressure.
- **Short sentences. No filler.** Every line on the site should either explain a benefit or move them toward the CTA. If it doesn't do either, cut it.
- **Coach-first framing.** Always position the coach as the expert, the AI as the infrastructure. Never imply the AI replaces them — it replaces the manual grunt work around them.
